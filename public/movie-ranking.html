Ash's favorite movies

My top 3 movies of all time!

The Godfather (1972)

A great crime film combined with family drama. Classic performances!

Akira (1988)

A cyberpunk anime set in a dystopic neo Tokyo. Great visuals and action!

Citizen Kane (1941)

An endearing movie of a media mogul, his astronmic rise to power and decline. A masterclass in visual storytelling!
